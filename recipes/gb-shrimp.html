Garlic Buttered Shrimp

Description

The Garlic Buttered shrimp is a dish reknowned for its savory and delicious taste worldwide.

One of the reasons why it's so popular is because it's considerably cheap to make, making one of the most popular

dishes to be made in all kinds of events including, but not limited to; Birthdays, Feast, Parties, etc...

Ingredients

  • Clean and deveined shrimp
  • butter
  • Garlic
  • Crushed red pepper flakes
  • Fresh lemon juice

Steps

  1. Season the shrimp with salt and set aside while you do the other prep.
  2. In a large stainless steel pan over medium heat, add the butter. Once the butter has melted and is bubbling, add the shrimp and lay flat, make sure each shrimp are touching the pan. Let it cook on the first side until the shrimp are half way cooked up the side, around 2-4 minutes.
  3. Add the garlic and red pepper flakes and begin stirring the shrimp and coating it withe the garlic and red pepper flakes. Flip the shrimp to the uncooked side and cook or 2-3 minutes. Once the shrimp are pink throughout, add the lemon juice and stir to combine.
  4. Serve with the butter lemon sauce from the pan, garnish with parsley and enjoy!
